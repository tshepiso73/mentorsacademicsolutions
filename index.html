ISSIP (INDEPENDENT SECONDARY SCHOOL IMPROVEMENT PROGRAMME )
ISSIP is a highly successful extra-mural supplementary tuition programme for learners in high schools
ISSIP is based on a centralised and standardised approach to how additional tuition is managed and delivered.
“Through the programme, Learners will receive additional training to ensure they are confident in approaching their exams.



ISSIP will be an important element in improving the quality of education in Gauteng schools by giving support to educators and school managers to ensure better curriculum delivery.
Under ISSIP, Grade 8-11 learners get extra classes in the afternoon during the weekdays and Saturdays , Sundays and during school holidays.
Extra classes for the GET and FET will run throughout the week. Once term starts, extra classes will be held everyday during the week In the afternoon including Saturday and Sunday . Programmes will also run during the June holidays, as well as the September vacation.
